Who We Are

Corcoran Lifestyle
Properties

We started Corcoran Lifestyle Properties with one mission: make quality homeownership accessible to everyone — not just those with perfect credit or a bank loan. Since 2015, we've helped hundreds of families across America get into homes they love, on terms they can actually afford.

We specialize in tiny homes, park model RVs, and single-section mobile homes — the most affordable path to real homeownership in today's market. Our in-house seller financing eliminates the bank middleman so we can say yes when others say no.

We're not just selling homes — we're changing lives. Every closing is a family stepping into something that's truly theirs.
