Eligibility

Do I Qualify?

We make it easy. Here's what helps — and what doesn't disqualify you.

✅ What Helps Your Application

  • ✓ Steady income (employment, self-employment, benefits, Social Security)
  • ✓ Ability to make a down payment ($1,500+)
  • ✓ Place to put the home (owned land, family land, park/community)
  • ✓ References available
  • ✓ Any credit score — even rebuilding
  • ✓ Co-signer if needed

❌ What Does NOT Disqualify You

  • ✗ Low credit score or no credit history
  • ✗ Past bankruptcy (if discharged)
  • ✗ Self-employment income
  • ✗ Limited savings
  • ✗ Previous repossession
  • ✗ Being a first-time buyer
